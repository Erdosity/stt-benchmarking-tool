Contact Center Transcription Benchmarking Calculator

According to Deloitte, 62% of companies view customer experience delivered by the contact centers as a competitive differentiator. In call centers, agents follow repetitive scripts that are not personalized to customers and their intents. However, using AI, businesses can model customer and agent interactions and streamline call routing and the overall customer engagement process using automation. Despite existing silos in contact centers, line of business specific criteria can be mapped into learning systems. This will result in a greater first call resolution and, consequently, a lower average handling time. Live agents can then be repurposed for higher touch or escalation related issues. In a 2017 McKinsey & Company survey of customer-care executives, 57% of executives considered call reduction their number-one priority for the next 5 years. By understanding why customers are contacting businesses this objective can be achieved. The Cognitive APIs highlighted in this tool can be leveraged to accelerate the path to contact center transformation.
