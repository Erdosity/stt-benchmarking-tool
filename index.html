An app for comparing Total Cost of Deployment of Speech-to-Text (STT) Services across cloud services including human costs
According to Deloitte, 62% of companies view customer experience delivered by the contact centers as a competitive differentiator. In a call center setting, agents often follow repetitive scripts that are not personalized to the customer and his or her call intent. However, using AI, businesses can model this customer and agent interaction and streamline call routing and the overall customer engagement process using automation. Despite existing silos in contact centers, line of business specific criteria can be mapped into learning systems. This will result in a greater first call resolution and, consequently, a lower average handling time. Live agents can then be repurposed for higher touch or escalation elated issues. In a 2017 survey of customer-care executives conducted by McKinsey & Company, 57% of executives considered call reduction their number-one priority for the next five years. By understanding why customers are contacting businesses this objective can be achieved. The Cognitive APIs highlighted in this tool can be leveraged to accelerate the path to contact center transformation.
